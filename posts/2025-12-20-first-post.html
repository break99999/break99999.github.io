I wanted something simple: a place to write that isn’t algorithmic, isn’t optimized, and doesn’t evaporate if a platform changes its mind.

“Make a place. Put things there. Leave them.” — an approach to the web I miss

This site is intentionally plain: static HTML, a stylesheet, and a folder of posts. If it lasts ten years, it succeeds.
